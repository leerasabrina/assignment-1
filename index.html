Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date: 05/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh launched its first satellite, Bangabandhu-1, on May 12, 2018 from the Kennedy Space Center in Florida, USA. The satellite was launched using a SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket. The satellite was used for broadcasting and communication. It was used by 40 private television stations, three state-run Bangladesh Television channels, and 26 foreign TV channels.
